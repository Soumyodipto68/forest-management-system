Official Jeep Safari Booking – India

Explore Indian National Parks through regulated Forest Department safaris

About Jeep Safari in India

Jeep Safari is the most widely used and government-approved method of wildlife tourism in India. Conducted inside National Parks and Tiger Reserves, these safaris operate under strict guidelines issued by State Forest Departments and the Ministry of Environment, Forest and Climate Change.

Safaris are conducted in open 4x4 vehicles accompanied by trained drivers and government-authorized naturalist guides.

How Safari Booking Works

  1. Select a National Park / Tiger Reserve
  2. Choose safari date and time slot
  3. Submit visitor details
  4. Forest Department verifies booking
  5. Safari permit issued (subject to availability)

🐅 Jim Corbett National Park (Uttarakhand)

Jim Corbett National Park is India’s oldest national park and the first Tiger Reserve under Project Tiger. It is famous for Bengal Tigers, elephants, and rich birdlife.

  • Safari Zones: Dhikala, Bijrani, Jhirna, Dhela
  • Best Time: November – June
  • Safari Type: Jeep Safari

🐯 Ranthambore National Park (Rajasthan)

Ranthambore is one of the most popular tiger reserves in India, known for its open landscapes and frequent tiger sightings. The park is also home to the historic Ranthambore Fort.

  • Safari Zones: Zone 1 to Zone 10
  • Best Time: October – June
  • Safari Type: Jeep Safari

🦏 Kaziranga National Park (Assam)

Kaziranga National Park is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and home to two-thirds of the world’s one-horned rhinoceros population.

  • Safari Zones: Central, Western, Eastern
  • Best Time: November – April
  • Safari Type: Jeep Safari
